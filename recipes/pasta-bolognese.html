Pasta Bolognese

Description

This pasta bolognese is inspired by a traditional italian recipe, but with some of my own modifications. Always use papardelle.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. 80/20 ground beef
  • ½ lb. ground pork
  • 1½ cups tomato sauce
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 glass white wine
  • 1 medium white/sweet onion
  • 2 large celery sticks
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 3 strips thick-cut bacon
  • 1½ lbs. papardelle pasta
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp oregano

Steps

Pasta should be cooked at the same time as the Chicken and Sauce.
