Pasta Bolognese

Description

This pasta bolognese is inspired by a traditional italian recipe, but with some of my own modifications. Always use papardelle.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. 80/20 ground beef
  • ½ lb. ground pork
  • 1½ cups tomato sauce
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 glass white wine
  • 1 medium white/sweet onion
  • 2 large celery sticks
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 3 strips thick-cut bacon
  • 1½ lbs. papardelle pasta
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp oregano

Steps

Pasta should be cooked at the same time as the Meat Sauce.

Pasta

  1. Put water in medium to large pot and set to the highest heat.
  2. Add a generous amount of salt. More than you think.
  3. Once boiling, add pasta to pot.
  4. Stir immediately, then once every minute until cooked
  5. Check you pasta brand's bag for estimated cooking time. Once that time has passed, take a bite of a noodle to see if it's done.
  6. Strain the pasta and keep it to the side.

Meat Sauce

  1. Chop onions, celery, and carrots into a small dice.
  2. Thinly mince the garlic.
  3. Cut bacon into thin lardons
  4. Set a medium to large pot to medium-high heat (7 out 10)
  5. Add the bacon to the pot and cook until the fat renders out, and you get a little bit of browning.
  6. Reduce heat to medium (5 out of 10) and add your chopped veggies (not the garlic yet).
  7. Keep mixing periodically for about 10 minutes, making sure nothing burns.
    *Raise heat if the pot is too quiet.*
  8. Add the beef and pork to the pot.
  9. Season with pepper, oregano, and a generous amount of salt.
  10. Raise heat to medium-high (7 outn of 10)
  11. After the meat browns, add the glass of white wine.
  12. Once the alcohol is cooked out (about 3 minutes), add tomato sauce.
  13. Add water until beef is completely submerged in liquid
  14. Reduce the heat to a simmer and let cook for about 1-1½ hours, checking in periodically, and adding water if it gets too dry.
  15. Make sure the sauce isn't watery, then add the cream.

Final Product

Finally, mix in your cooked pasta and enjoy!
