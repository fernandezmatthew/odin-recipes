Cajun Pasta

Description

This spicy cajun chicken pasta is sure to tantalize your tastebuds without taking all day to prepare or breaking the bank!

Ingredients

  • 1½-2 lbs. of bonesless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 medium white/sweet onion
  • 1 large green bell pepper
  • 2 large celery sticks
  • ¼ cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 lb. dried penne pasta
  • ¼ cup cajun seasoning
  • 1 tbsp. pepper
  • 1 gallon water
  • salt to taste
