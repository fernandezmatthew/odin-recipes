Cajun Pasta

Description

This spicy cajun chicken pasta is sure to tantalize your tastebuds without taking all day to prepare or breaking the bank!

Ingredients

  • 1½-2 lbs. of bonesless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 medium white/sweet onion
  • 1 large green bell pepper
  • 2 large celery sticks
  • ¼ cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 lb. dried penne pasta
  • ¼ cup cajun seasoning
  • 1 tbsp. pepper
  • 1 gallon water
  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • salt to taste

Steps

Pasta should be cooked at the same time as the Chicken and Sauce.

Sauce should be prepped before chicken is done cooking, but not cooked until after the chicken is done.

Pasta

  1. Put water in medium to large pot and set to the highest heat.
  2. Add a generous amount of salt. More than you think.
  3. Once boiling, add pasta to pot.
  4. Stir immediately, then once every 5 minutes until cooked
  5. Check you pasta brand's box for estimated cooking time. Once that time has passed, take a bite of a noodle to see if it's done.
  6. Strain the pasta and keep it to the side.

Chicken

  1. Cut chicken into bite size chunks.
  2. Place the chicken in a bowl along with 1 tbsp. salt and all cajun seasoning.
  3. Mix until seasoning is evenly distributed.
  4. Set a large saute pan on medium-high heat (7 out of 10) and add 1 tbsp. olive oil.
  5. Once pan is at about 200°C, add the chicken in, evenly spreading on the surface of the pan.
  6. After about 3½ minutes, flip all the chicken pieces.
  7. After another 3 minutes, taste to ensure cookness, and remove from pan into a bowl.
  8. Reduce pan heat to medium (5 out of 10).

Sauce

  1. Chop onions, celery, and bell pepper into a small dice.
  2. Add 2 tbsp. of olive oil to the pan.
  3. Add veggies to the pan after it's cooled down to medium heat.
  4. Add a fair amount of salt, and all pepper.
  5. Keep mixing periodically for about 10 minutes, making sure nothing burns.
    *Raise heat if the pan is too quiet.*
  6. Once veggies are translucent and a little sweet, mix in all tomato sauce.
  7. Add a little more salt to taste and let cook for about 5 minutes.
  8. Mix in chicken and all cream
  9. Once sauce starts to thicken a little bit, mix in all cheese and salt to taste.

Final Product

Finally, mix in your cooked pasta, turn off the heat, and enjoy!
